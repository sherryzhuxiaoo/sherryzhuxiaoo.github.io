I've got this one daring wish I've been itching to check off my list - skydiving!

Since childhood, I had always wanted to try skydiving, but my mom wasn't supportive. Every time I brought it up, she would say, "Try it next time."

As the years went by, I postponed it until I turned eighteen. At that point, I saw it as a challenge for my coming of age.

However, when I arrived at the skydiving location, I hesitated and ultimately decided not to proceed.

Now, during the last month of my nineteenth year, I wanted to embark on a new journey before turning twenty. So, I headed to Miami this winter break and, in Key West, finally realized my dream of skydiving.
